# Doctolib — Executive Summary

## Company at a Glance

- **Founded**: 2013, Paris
- **Founders**: Stanislas Niox-Chateau (CEO), Ivan Schneider, Jessy Bernal
- **HQ**: Levallois-Perret (Paris suburb), France
- **Valuation**: €5.8B Series G (Jan 2022) → €3.6B implied in Q1 2026 secondary (–40%, but ARR +56%)
- **ARR**: ~€270M (2022) → €348M (2024) → ~€422M (2025) — operational profitability confirmed
- **R&D spend**: €115M–€150M/year
- **Employees**: ~2,800–3,100 (uncertainty flagged across sources)
- **Markets**: France (dominant), Germany, Italy — UK entry via Medicus AI acquisition (May 2026)
- **Business model**: SaaS subscription for healthcare professionals; free for patients

## Scale

- **Patients**: 70M+ registered accounts
- **Healthcare professionals**: 350,000+ practitioners
- **Appointments**: 100M+ booked per year
- **Specialties**: GP, dentist, ophthalmologist, gynaecologist, physio, nurse, 50+
- **No-show rate**: ~2% (vs industry standard ~15%)

## Core Products

### Patient-side
- **Booking app**: Search/book by specialty, location, availability
- **Patient portal**: Medical history, upcoming appointments, reminders, document upload
- **Teleconsultation**: Video (launched COVID-19, 2020)
- **Vaccination campaigns**: COVID vaccine scheduling (2021) — proved infrastructure scale

### Professional (Pro) side
- **Agenda management**: Digital calendar, replaces phone booking
- **Patient records (DMI/EHR)**: Electronic health records linked to bookings, OCR document classification
- **Financial & billing**: Digital invoices, ICD-10/EBM coding suggestions, Payment Guarantee
- **Messaging (Siilo acquisition)**: Secure inter-professional messaging for tele-expertise

## AI Strategy — The "Operating System for Healthcare" Thesis

Doctolib's strategic shift: **scheduling infrastructure → AI-native clinical workflow partner**.

**Published roadmap:**
| Date | Feature | Key facts |
|---|---|---|
| Oct 2024 | AI Consultation Assistant | Ambient AI scribe. 1M+ consultations in first months. 50% documentation time cut. Audio deleted in 48h. Human-in-the-loop required. |
| May 2025 | Spring AI Acceleration | Medical Dictation, Automatic Referral Letter Generation |
| Dec 2025 | AI Telephone Assistant | Aaron.ai acquisition built in. 80% of routine calls handled autonomously. 200K+ calls by spring 2026. Saves ~6h/week. |
| May 2026 | UK expansion / Medicus acquisition | £100M investment, London R&D center, 150 hires |
| Ongoing | Inria joint lab | €20M, sovereign AI, "general medical intelligence system" goal |

**AI philosophy (CEO public statements):**
- AI augments physicians, never replaces. "The caregiver remains in control of everything."
- Privacy-first: audio transcription deleted within 48h; never sold
- Sovereign AI: Europe should build its own clinical AI without US/Chinese dependency
- "I look the patient in the eyes again" — admin reduction as the real KPI

## Design System Signal

- 50+ person international design team
- Published mandate: **"evolve the Doctolib Design System to inherently support agentic AI components"**
- Current gap: no confirmed AI-native primitives (conversational UI, provenance indicators, consent gates)
- The pre-consultation history feature requires exactly these primitives on both patient and pro surfaces

## Competitive Moat

- Integrated workflow: calendar + EHR + billing + AI in one vs. standalone AI scribes (Nabla, Corti)
- Network effects: more patients → more practitioners → better availability
- Government partnerships (COVID vaccines) built institutional trust and scale proof
- UK: Medicus acquisition bypassed 25-year NHS regulatory lock-in (EMIS/TPP duopoly)
